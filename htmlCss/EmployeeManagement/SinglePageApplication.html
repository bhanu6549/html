About Us

Mindtree Limited is an Indian multinational information technology and outsourcing company headquartered in Bangalore, India and New Jersey, USA. It is a Larsen & Toubro group company. Founded in 1999, the company employs approximately 20,204 employees with annual revenue of $1 Billion.
